Buttons

Buttons should always contain actions. The language should be clear and concise. Capitalise only the first word. Standard buttons on our site include:

Sign the petition

Subscribe

Donate

Read more

These verbs have also been found to work well in calls to action:

Expose

Spread the word

Raise your voice

Unite

Support

Join
